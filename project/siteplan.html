Crystal Clear Detail

This is a business that specializes in interior and exterior auto detailing services.

Site Purpose

This site provides will provide information about the detailing services offered and entice visitors to schedule an appointment to get their vehicle detailed.

Scenarios

“What specific detailing services are offered, and what is included in each package?”

“How does the interior detailing process differ from the exterior detailing?"

“Are mobile or on-site detailing options available?”

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: Deep Blue: #001F3F - Will be used for headings, navigation, and call-to-action.
  • Secondary Color: Silver: #C0C0C0 - Will be used for sub-headings, accents, borders, and icons.
  • Background Color: White: #FFFFFF - Will provide clean background for readability and it won't detract from other aspects of the site.

Typography

  • Montserrat: Headings, navigation, hero
  • Roboto: Body, descriptions, general
  • Lato: Call-to-action, quotes
